A History of Surfing

Introduction

Surfing is a surface water sport in which a person (the surfer) rides a board (the surfboard) on the forward face of a wave as it carries the surfer towards the shore. The energy from the wave gives the surfer momentum, propelling them forward. Surfing is believed to have originated in ancient Polynesia and has evolved into a popular global sport with a rich cultural history.
